An electric fence, often used to keep animals confined to a pasture, can be powered by a 12 V car battery. The voltage is increased to around 1 kV using a DC-DC Converter and the output from the DC-DC converter is applied between a wire that runs along the top of the fence and earth ground. This video demonstrates electrical insulation in action and how to have fun with DC-DC converters and an electric fence.
